This folder contains Count analyses for direct comparison to ALE reconstructions in 
* Williams et al 2017 [10.1073/pnas.1618463114] Dataset at https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.5172310.v3
* Harris et al. 2022 [10.1038/s41559-022-01885-x] Dataset at https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.c.5682706.v1
* Coleman et al 2021 [10.1126/science.abe0511] Dataset at https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.12651074.v12

ALE files (branchwise event sums) from the original datasets as indicated.

Coleman2021-recmcl-t398-families.txt shows family statistics over MCL data and species tree 398: alignment lengths and parsimony informative sites; per-family copy losses, duplications, transfers and acquisitions (transfer + 1 origination).
